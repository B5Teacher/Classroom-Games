Word Chain

Weather & Emotions · type directly on the dashes

How to play

Each chain has 5 words. Every word starts with the last letter of the one before.
Gold = vocabulary  ·  Blue = bridge word.

Example
① foggy → ends Y
② yell → ends L
③ lightning → ends G
④ grab → ends B
⑤ bored

5 wrong guesses per word ends the game. Just start typing — letters appear on the dashes.
