Word Chain

Every word starts with the last letter of the one before

How to play

Each chain has 5 words. Words 1, 3, 5 are vocabulary; words 2 and 4 are bridge words.
The first letter of each word is always given. 5 wrong guesses ends the game.

Example
① foggy → ends Y
② yell → ends L
③ lightning → ends G
④ grab → ends B
⑤ bored
■ vocab   ■ bridge   first letter always shown
Needs at least 3 words. Chains are generated automatically each game.
